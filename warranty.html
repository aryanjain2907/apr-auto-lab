Warranty Terms & Conditions

What's Covered

  • Defects in materials and workmanship
  • Premature failure of installed parts
  • Issues resulting from our service work
  • Manufacturer defects in products we install

What's Not Covered

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Damage from accidents or misuse
  • Modifications made by other parties
  • Damage from environmental factors beyond our control
  • Services performed without proper maintenance

Warranty Requirements

  • Keep your original service receipt
  • Follow recommended maintenance schedules
  • Use our facility or authorized service centers for related work
  • Report issues promptly within warranty period

Transferability

Most warranties are transferable to new owners with proper documentation. Contact us for transfer procedures.

Questions?
