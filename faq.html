Detailing Services

How long does a detailing service take?

▼
Service duration depends on the package selected. Basic detailing takes 2-3 hours, Premium detailing takes 4-6 hours, and Ultimate detailing with ceramic coating can take 8-12 hours. We'll provide an accurate timeline when you book.

How often should I get my car detailed?

▼
We recommend a basic detail every 3-4 months, premium detailing every 6 months, and ceramic coating every 2-3 years. However, this depends on your usage, climate, and personal preferences.

What is ceramic coating and how long does it last?

▼
Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that bonds with your vehicle's paint, creating a protective layer. It provides superior protection against UV rays, oxidation, and minor scratches. When properly maintained, it can last 2-5 years.

Engine Services

What types of engine services do you provide?

▼
We offer comprehensive engine services including diagnostics, oil changes, filter replacements, timing belt service, head gasket repair, engine rebuilds, performance tuning, and complete engine overhauls.

Do you provide warranties on engine work?

▼
Yes, all our engine services come with a warranty. Basic services include a 90-day warranty, while major repairs and rebuilds come with a 12-month warranty. Warranty details are provided with your service receipt.

How do I know if my engine needs service?

▼
Warning signs include unusual noises, check engine light, reduced performance, excessive smoke, oil leaks, or if you've exceeded the recommended service interval. We offer free diagnostic checks to identify any issues.
